I Am
National Public School Kalkere

National public school, Kalkere, in keeping with the merits of true education as outlined by great visionaries, is the latest addition to the NPS group of schools. The NPS family of schools is indubitably the best in Bangalore, following innovative methods in educational pedagogy. The school which follows CBSE syllabus, has a state-of-the-art campus comprising of a well-spaced scholastic block, aesthetically designed creative center with separate rooms for art, dance, music and yoga, a spacious conference hall and convention center, athletic & sport facilities. NPS Kalkere is committed to imparting high quality holistic education that aims at creating responsible citizens of tomorrow, by giving students the opportunities to develop multi-faceted skills and abilities, through a wide variety of programmes in a caring, innovative and nurturing environment. Students and faculty are encouraged to convert every challenge into opportunity by following our school motto - reach out, reach high and reach beyond.
